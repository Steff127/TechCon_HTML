Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a local techmeet up and has since evolved into a global conference drawing thousands of tech enthusiasts and professionals each year. From humble beginings to a major international event, TechCon continues to foster innovation and collaboration.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire, educate, and empower technology professionals around the world by providing a platform for knowledge sharing and networking. TechCon is committed to inclusivity, accessibility, and cutting-edge discussions.

Notable Past Speakers

Ada Lovelace Renowned computer scientist known for her foundational work in programming logic and theory.

Alan Turing Mathematician and cryptanalyst whose work laid the groundwork for modern computing and AI.

Grace Hopper Innovator in programming languages and computer science education, inspiring generations of tech leaders.
